About the baithak

A baithak is a room you are invited to sit in — for music, for talk, for poetry. This one belongs to temporal, writing from Toronto since 2006: daily haiku and senryu, transliterated Urdu ghazals, narrative prose poems, and the occasional political verse or “poessay.”

Every piece here is drawn live from the blog itself, so the room is always complete and always current.
